About me

Who Am I?

Hi, my name is Eliza Casapopol! I am driven to help organizations and individuals thrive with the help of artificial intelligence. In my role as an AI & data engineer I work closely with data scientists, software engineers and business stakeholders to build end-to-end AI applications that change the status quo. I have gained significant expertise in enabling international companies and scale-ups in becoming more data-driven: bringing models in the production environment where the decision-makers can interact with their output, creating interactive AI applications and automating data analysis.

I am passionate to teach others about the power of technology. Thus, I have developed courses and trained professionals from international companies on various topics in data and technology. Being determined to increase digital literacy, I work to inspire children to follow a career in STEM. I co-founded the first national science festival in my home country, Romania, to inspire people of all ages to gain a deeper understanding of science and technology.

I am a strong advocate for gender diversity and inclusion in STEM. I have spoken about AI and diversity at numerous conferences, such as Women in Tech Regatta, and am the Diversity & Inclusion lead for the PyData Amsterdam community.
